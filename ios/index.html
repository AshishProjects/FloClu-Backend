About

FloClu is an application which helps in educating women who do not know much about their mesntrual cycle and body changes. This application acts like an assistant to track their menstrual cycle and are reminded to keep menstrual products with them before their cycle starts. This application also has a feature called 'Alert' where if the user needs any menstrual product can send alert to users and take help from users nearby.

This application is used for supporting and talking to the teenagers. The concept revolves around person's health, in order to provide better solutions to people who are not comfortable in talking/sharing or reaching out to others about their problems.

Features

FloClu provides various features to help every women in need.These set of features are carefully designed for women to help them in day to day life and make their period days much easy.

Tracking

This feature helps the user to keep track of their menstrual cycle.It keeps log of the start day, end day and number of days of the cycle.

Alert

Reach out to nearby people and get menstruation product. Send an alert by using alert feature.

Location

Send and know about the location of person in need and the person helping the one in need.

Post

Users will be able to create posts asking for solutions or sharing their own experience on which other users can comment.
